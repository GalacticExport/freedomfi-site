FAQ

Where do I find the official Contract Address?

It will be shown in the Contract Address box on the homepage with a one-tap copy button.

Why does the site show “—” for Holders?

Live holders usually requires a data provider key (Birdeye or similar). When you add a key, holders will update automatically. We don’t show fake numbers.

Which chart is used?

Dexscreener. The site automatically selects the best pair for your token using their API.

Is this financial advice?

No. This site is informational and community-focused. Always do your own research.
